Frequently Asked Questions

  • Are the meditation sessions and 'Lunar Jams' included in the cost? expand_more

    Yes. All activities at the resort are included in the cost of a room.

  • Does the resort provide any vegan/vegetarian food options? expand_more

    Yes. It's 2020, duh. Our wonderful team of chefs will cater to any dietary needs. Just make sure to state your any restrictions on your booking.

  • Are the medical facilities at the resort in case something happens? expand_more

    Yes. Due to the sometimes wild nature of the Lunar Jams, as well as other unexpected accidents, we have a professional medical team on site 24/7. In addition, the resort will cover any medical costs for guests.

  • What is the temperature on the moon? What kind of clothing should I bring? expand_more

    Due to the lack of oxygen on the moon, all guests will be provided with comfortable, custom-made space suits to be worn for the duration of the trip.

  • Are there aliens on the moon? expand_more

    Many guests have reported alien sightings late in the evenings. These sightings usually occur after the Lunar Jams, leading us to believe there might be other forces at play.

When Lunar Aural was founded, we had one goal in mind. We wanted to bridge the gap between the known and the unknown. We wanted to give man an outlet for his curiosity.

As humans, we spend a lot of our time thinking about the past and the future. In doing so, we often ignore the intricacies and the purity of life in this moment.

It is only the present that holds the infinite wisdom we crave. In its grasp we realize that the division between what we know and do not know only existed in the confine of our own mind.

At Lunar Aural, we want to help you lose your mind. We want you to experience the absolute bliss that pulses behind the facade of our understanding. Sounds insane, right? Exactly.

It's all about the senses. Your sound and your sight. Your eyes will see and your eyes will hear what your mind cannot comprehend. A sky overflowing with stars. A song played by the mystics. And then..

Complete silence. Complete darkness. Nothing but yourself. Welcome to Lunar Aural. You have been waiting your whole life for this moment. May we interest you in a dose of real life?
